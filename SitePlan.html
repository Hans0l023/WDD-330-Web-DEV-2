Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help those who are starting their work out journey or anybody who is already working out to up the exercise game. This web site will give a list of exercises and will be broken down by type of work out.

Audience

The audience is anybody looking for workout ideas, it can be challenging to know where to start the journey or how to mix up your routine. This site will help with that.

Major Functions

Major functions will include a breakdown of the type of workout desired such as cardio or powerlifting. There are seven different types of workouts from this API. Once you select type a list will show up of the workouts that fall under that list. There will be a filter by difficulty on that page. Each workout will show what muscle it works. Clicking on one of those individual workouts will then show all of the details of the workout.
